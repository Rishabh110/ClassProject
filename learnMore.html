What Free Agent is All About


Never Let That Craving Fade Away

Some of my most memorable moments of my young adulthood was when I was jamming with friends. Almost every weekend we would get together, let loose and connect on a level only musican's know of. As the years passed, the jam sessions became less and less and I found my self finding that I was craving a jam session. It didn't even matter who with, I just wanted that connectivity and the feeling of spontaneous creation.

This App is the solution to that problem. No matter where you are, or what day of the week it is, you can find that person to jam with. A profile will be made in which you can specify the genre of music you're inclined to, and your instrument(s) and the appropriate skill level with that instrument. It can be an app that lets you scratch that itch once in a while, or it could be the app that helps you create that band you have always been looking for.


Jammer's Nearby

The Application will automatically sync and find other Free Agent's nearby that are active and looking to have a session.

Easily Accessible Free Agent Info

Free Agent's Profiles will be easily accessible for you to evaluate whether or not you could make a great fit.
